# Open Graph Image Guide

## What You Need

You need to create a **1200 x 630 pixels** image for social media sharing (Open Graph).

## Image Specifications

- **Dimensions**: 1200px width × 630px height (exactly)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File Name**: `helxon-og-image.jpg`
- **Location**: `/public/helxon-og-image.jpg`
- **File Size**: Aim for under 1MB (preferably 300-500KB)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 1.91:1

## Content Suggestions

Your Open Graph image should include:

1. **Helxon Logo** - Prominently displayed
2. **Tagline** - Something like:
   - "Enterprise Cybersecurity Solutions"
   - "SOC-as-a-Service"
   - "24/7 Threat Monitoring"
3. **Background** - Professional, tech-focused design
4. **Color Scheme** - Match your brand colors (appears to be dark/professional theme)
5. **Visual Elements** - Consider adding:
   - Shield icons
   - Network/connection graphics
   - Lock/security symbols
   - Tech/circuit patterns

## Design Tips

### Safe Zone
- Keep important text and logos at least **40px from all edges**
- Center your main message
- Avoid critical information in corners (may be cropped on some platforms)

### Text
- Use large, bold, readable fonts
- Maximum 2-3 short lines of text
- High contrast (light text on dark background or vice versa)
- Font size: At least 60-80px for main text

### Branding
- Include your logo (200-300px wide recommended)
- Use brand colors consistently
- Professional, clean design

1. Place it in `/public/helxon-og-image.jpg`

## Quick Canva Template Steps

1. Go to Canva.com
2. Search for "Open Graph" or create custom size (1200 x 630)
3. Add your logo
4. Add background (gradient, solid color, or image)
5. Add text: Company name and tagline
6. Add visual elements (icons, shapes)
7. Download as JPG or PNG
8. Save as `helxon-og-image.jpg` in `/public/` folder

## Alternative: Use Existing Marketing Material
